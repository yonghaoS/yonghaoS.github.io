About Me

Building interactive systems from materials, data, and everyday environments.

My work sits at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction, computational materials, smart environments, and machine learning applications. I am especially interested in transforming common physical surfaces such as wood panels, furniture, and room-scale structures into sensing and actuation interfaces.

Before joining SFU, I worked as a software engineer and project manager at the Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and completed degrees in computer science, data science, and electrical and electronic engineering in the United Kingdom and China.
